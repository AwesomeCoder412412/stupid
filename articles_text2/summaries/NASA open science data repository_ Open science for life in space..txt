The NASA Open Science Data Repository (OSDR) enhances accessibility to critical space biology and health data essential for deep space missions. Integrating the Ames Life Sciences Data Archive with GeneLab, OSDR promotes open science principles, ensuring that data remains findable, accessible, interoperable, reusable, and reproducible. By providing a diverse array of life sciences data alongside tools for submission, retrieval, and analysis, OSDR fosters global collaboration and inclusivity in space biology research, significantly contributing to understanding the biological impacts of spaceflight on humans and other organisms.
