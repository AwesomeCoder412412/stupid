The review discusses SARS-CoV-2 and its impact on COVID-19, highlighting the virus's mechanism of infection and its ability to manipulate the intracellular environment for replication. It emphasizes the increasing global cases and deaths attributed to COVID-19, the significance of ongoing viral mutations, and the need for comprehensive research to understand these dynamics and their clinical implications. This is part one of a three-part series aimed at improving understanding of the virus, its effects on human biology, and future therapeutic options.
