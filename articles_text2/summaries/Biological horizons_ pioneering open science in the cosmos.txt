The article "Biological horizons: pioneering open science in the cosmos" discusses NASA's efforts to promote open science in biological and biomedical research related to space exploration. The NASA Open Science Data Repository (OSDR) provides valuable access to diverse datasets from spaceflight experiments, enriching research through collaboration and standardization, all aimed at enhancing scientific discovery. With the launch of the Transform to Open Science program (TOPS), NASA is focusing on reproducibility and inclusivity in scientific research, significantly benefiting both space missions and human health on Earth.
