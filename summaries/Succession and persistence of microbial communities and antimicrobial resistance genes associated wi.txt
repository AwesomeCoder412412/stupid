The text is a correction notice for the article "Succession and persistence of microbial communities and antimicrobial resistance genes associated with International Space Station environmental surfaces." The authors acknowledged a typographical error in the scientific notation regarding the number of reads generated, clarifying that approximately 7.3 × 10^8 reads were produced from the samples, with the normalized total being 3.1 × 10^8 reads across all samples. They expressed regret for any confusion caused by this mistake.
