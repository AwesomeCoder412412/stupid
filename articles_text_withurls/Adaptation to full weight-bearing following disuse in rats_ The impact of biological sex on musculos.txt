Adaptation to full weight‐bearing following disuse in rats: The impact of biological sex on musculoskeletal recovery
", e15938.
Issertine, M., Rosa‐Caldwell, M. E., Sung, D.‐M., Bouxsein, M. L., Rutkove, S. B., & Mortreux, M. (2024). Adaptation to full weight‐bearing following disuse in rats: The impact of biological sex on musculoskeletal recovery.
